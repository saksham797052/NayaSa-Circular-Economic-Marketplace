About NayaSa

NayaSa is a circular economy marketplace that empowers individuals and communities to donate, upcycle, and extend the life of products. Our mission is to reduce waste by making reuse and repair easier, accessible, and more joyful.

Our Vision

To build a zero-waste society powered by sustainable exchanges.

Our Mission

We aim to create opportunities for people to reuse, repair, and repurpose products instead of discarding them.

Our Community

From individual donors to passionate recyclers, our community shares a passion for sustainability.
